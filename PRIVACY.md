# Privacy Policy — Ai Notice

**Last updated:** 18 June 2026

Ai Notice is a browser extension that inspects files and pasted content locally, on your device, before that content reaches third-party AI tools (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and similar services). If sensitive content is detected, Ai Notice pauses and warns you so you can decide whether to proceed.

This policy explains, in plain terms, what Ai Notice does and does not do with your data.

## The short version

Ai Notice does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any of your data. All processing happens entirely on your device. There is no server, no cloud component, no analytics, and no telemetry of any kind. Ai Notice's inspection logic does not require any Ai Notice server and makes no network requests to Ai Notice.

## What data Ai Notice processes

To do its job, Ai Notice inspects the content you are about to send to an AI tool — the text you paste and the files you attach — at the moment you interact with a supported AI service in your browser.

This inspection happens **locally, in your browser, in memory**. The content is analysed on the spot to check for sensitive material. It is **not** copied to disk by Ai Notice, **not** sent anywhere, and **not** retained after the check completes.

## What Ai Notice does NOT do

- **No data collection.** Ai Notice does not collect personal information, browsing history, page contents, the content it inspects, or any usage data.
- **No transmission.** Nothing you paste, upload, or type leaves your device because of Ai Notice. The extension makes no network requests to any Ai Notice server — there is no Ai Notice server.
- **No telemetry or analytics.** There is no usage tracking, crash reporting, fingerprinting, behavioural analytics, or identifiers of any kind.
- **No third-party sharing.** Because no data is collected, there is nothing to share or sell. Ai Notice does not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.
- **No remote code.** Ai Notice does not load or execute code from any remote source. All code is contained within the published extension package.

## Data stored on your device

Ai Notice stores a small amount of data locally on your device only, using the browser's built-in extension storage. This may include settings and preferences, such as enabled rules or local configuration choices.

Ai Notice does not store the text you paste or the contents of files you attach as part of this settings data. Local settings data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the extension.

## Licence validation

Enterprise editions of Ai Notice may validate licences offline on your device using cryptographic signature verification. Licence validation does not require a network connection and does not transmit information about you or your organisation to Ai Notice or any third party.

## Permissions

Ai Notice requests the permissions required to run its local warning and inspection logic in the browser.

| Permission | Why Ai Notice needs it |
|---|---|
| Host access to HTTPS websites | Ai Notice needs host access so it can detect supported web-based AI tools and show local warnings before pasted text or attached files are shared. This access is used only for local page detection and local warning behavior. Ai Notice does not use host access to collect browsing history, track browsing activity, or send page contents anywhere. |
| `storage` | Ai Notice uses browser extension storage to save settings and preferences locally on your device. This data is not sent to Ai Notice or any third party. |

Ai Notice does not request access to your browsing history, downloads, cookies, tabs history, or unrelated account data. It does not transmit pasted text, attached files, inspected content, settings, licence information, telemetry, or analytics to any Ai Notice server.

## Third-party AI services

Ai Notice helps you review content before you choose to send it to third-party AI services. If you decide to proceed and submit content to a service such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or another website, that service may process the content according to its own privacy policy and terms. Ai Notice does not control those third-party services.

## Children's privacy

Ai Notice is a professional security tool intended for adult and organisational use. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Because Ai Notice collects no data, any future changes will relate to clarity or new features rather than new data practices.
